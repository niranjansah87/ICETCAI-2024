Call for Papers

Authors are invited to submit original and unpublished research papers with an innovative idea on topics within the broad areas (but not limited to): data analysis, predictive insights, automation, and intelligent decision-making, Optimization, Mathematical Modelling, Numerical Methods & Algorithms, Neural Networks, Machine learning algorithms, Risk and compliance management, Web filtering, Access management, Data loss prevention, Encryption, interrelated computing devices, mechanical and digital machines, objects, animals and people. etc. Authors will be informed about the acceptance of their papers based on the recommendations of the peer review committee.
